Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Technical, security, and workflow insights.

What specific EMR/EHR systems do you support? +
Our team is experienced with Meditech, Connect Care (Alberta's provincial system), and Athena. We can also build secure workflows for custom or niche systems within 5–10 business days.
Is StarFait Data Labs HIA and HIPAA compliant?+
Absolutely. As an Alberta-based Information Manager under HIA, our processes meet HIPAA and PIPA requirements. Data residency in Canada.
What is the standard turnaround time for patient intake processing?+
Standard intake is completed within 12–24 hours. Expedited 4-hour processing available for urgent clinical needs.
How do patients submit their information securely?+
Through our encrypted Digital Intake Portal. Data is encrypted at submission and never stored on local unencrypted devices.
Do you provide Business Associate Agreements (BAA)?+
Yes, we provide a standard BAA for all clinical partners and maintain BAAs with our infrastructure providers for full chain of compliance.
